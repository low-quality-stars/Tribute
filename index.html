David Tennant

Summary

David John Tennant is a 52 y/o Scottish actor. His rise to fame began with his role as the 10th reincarnation of the doctor in Doctor Who. He has played a variety of different roles such as Barty Crouch Jr from Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire, Crowley from Good Omens, and Scrooge McDuck in DuckTales (2017-2021).

Some Things About Him

  • As a child David Tennant stated he wanted to be an actor because he was a fan of Doctor Who
  • Tennant made his debut in an anti-smoking film when he was 16
  • He was awarded his first major role in Takin' Over the Asylum in 1994
  • He developed his career through the British theatre, most often performing with the Royal Shakespeare Company
  • Tennant then began his run with Doctor Who
  • After his role as the 10th Doctor his career really started taking off
  • He met his wife in 2008 during the filming of the Doctor Who episode "The Doctor's Daughter"
  • He and his wife, Moffette, married in December of 2011
  • Tennant adopted Moffette's son Ty whom she had from a previous relationship
  • Since then they have had 4 other children, Olive, Wilfred, Doris, and Birdie
